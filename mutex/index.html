About MUTEX

MUTEX is the biggest scientific and technical annual event hosted by IEEE Zagazig Student Branch. The event mainly focuses on introducing the latest technologies in computer science fields to the audience and setting a clear path for them to follow.

Vision

Banishing the gap between the latest technology trends and the current community awareness.

Mission

Establishing a link between the leading companies/organizations in latest technologies and the academic students through sets of talks, trainings and workshops.

Goals

Spreading technological awareness among college students. Giving students work experience in the latest technologies through workshops. Finding well performing students and give them the chance to be interns in the company.

What to Expect at MUTEX

Learning

Learn from the leading multinational companies and well established organizations about technology trends and tech-related entrepreneurship topics through many talks and panel discussions.

Practising

Practice in hands-on/interactive workshops to gain practical experience

Networking

Meet Enterprise, Startups, Investors, and Techies who will all gather in one inspiring space for two days and connect with the Tech ecosystem.

Pitching

Are you a Tech or Tech-enabled startup? This is an opportunity to fund your business and connect with investors, gain coverage in media publications, and extend to potential customers in Al-Delta region. And also an opportunity to apply in our pitching competitions.
